The Integration Class

This is the TLM system that converts church visitors into committed members

Objectives Of The Integration Class

  • The programme offers a sequence of operations that track and follow up first timers till they are absorbed into the church family
  • The main objective of the programme is to offer an officia point of entry for everyone interested in becoming a part of TLM Church System.
  • It also serves to put new attendees on the track of growing into the fullnes of their destiny in God as disciples of Christ.
  • It is expected that during the teaching sesions, participants are given good understanding of what being a christian is about, the role that TLM will play in their lives as well as their roles as members of the Church Family

Treasured Believers School

This is a 6 weeks in-house programme for new converts as well as those want to go deeper in their walk of faith.

Objectives of TBS

  • To be a nurturing center where new believers can learn about their new life in Christ.
  • To be a place where new converts are with the milk of the word so that they will grow.
  • To be a point where converts are urshered to encounter the person and recieve the baptism of the HolyGhost
  • To be a stop over where Christians learn about the foundational principles of the Christian walk.
  • To be a draft center where believers are taught and encouraged to share their faith.
  • To give a refresher experience for anyone who desires to go deeper in the things of God.

There will also be an avenue for everyone interested and shortlisted to recieve water baptism
